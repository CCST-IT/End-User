How This Exercise Works

This is an end‑user communication and security training exercise. You will:

  • Read realistic email scenarios (including rude/defensive ones).
  • Choose the best response based on tone, clarity, and security.
  • Earn score, XP, and coins for strong choices.
  • See feedback explaining why each option is better or worse.
  • Reflect briefly on what you learned.

Use Career Mode for long practice, Boss Fight for intense escalations, and Final Exam for a focused assessment.
